Guidelines
Do
✓ Use primary on white or light backgrounds (#f8f9fb and above)
✓ Use white on dark (#283743, #10131d) or colored (#0051ff, #0d44ad) backgrounds
✓ Maintain clear space of at least 16px on all sides
✓ Minimum width: 48px (AR) · 100px (EN)
✓ Switch to lang="en" when the UI language is English
Don't
✕ Redraw, approximate, or rasterize the logo
✕ Place the primary variant on colored or dark backgrounds
✕ Add drop shadows, outlines, gradients, or any effects
✕ Stretch, skew, or alter proportions
✕ Use any color other than #0D44AD or white
